Welcome

Teewars Legacy INDEV

Teewars Legacy is a free online deathmatch game inspired by Teeworlds and built with an oldschool 2000s open source spirit. Control a small tee, move fast, hook around maps, shoot rivals, and chase a bigger score.

The project focuses on a classic source layout, pixelated graphics, simple rules, and a clear arcade feel.

About The Game

Teewars Legacy is an online deathmatch game licensed under the zlib license. You can download it, play indefinitely, and follow the source as it changes.

  • Oldschool source structure like 2000s games.
  • Oldschool pixelated graphics.
  • Two excellent maps.
